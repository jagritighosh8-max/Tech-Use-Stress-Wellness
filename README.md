# Tech-Use-Stress-Wellness
This dataset explores the connections between how people use technology in their daily lives and their overall well-being. It brings together information on screen time, social media and entertainment habits, sleep patterns, lifestyle choices, and different indicators of mental health such as mood, stress, anxiety, and depression.
About the Dataset

The dataset includes:

1. Technology Use

Screen time

Laptop/phone usage

Social media usage

Entertainment time (videos, games, etc.)

2. Lifestyle & Habits

Sleep duration & quality

Physical activity

Mindfulness practices

Diet & caffeine intake

3. Mental Health Indicators

Stress levels

Mood

Anxiety

Depression

Overall mental wellness score

This dataset helps answer questions like:

Does screen time affect stress and sleep?

Is social media linked to mood or anxiety?

Do healthy habits improve wellness despite heavy tech use?
